## Worked solution

Rewrite all bases as powers of \(3\):

\[
9^{2n+1}=3^{4n+2}
\]

\[
27^{2-n}=3^{6-3n}
\]

So

\[
\frac{9^{2n+1}\times 3^{4-3n}}{27^{2-n}}
=3^{(4n+2)+(4-3n)-(6-3n)}
=3^{4n}
\]

## Quickest method

Convert everything to base \(3\), add powers in the numerator and subtract the denominator power.

## Common traps

Subtracting only \(6\), instead of subtracting the whole denominator exponent \(6-3n\).
