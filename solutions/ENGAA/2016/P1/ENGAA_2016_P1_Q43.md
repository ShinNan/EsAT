## Worked solution

Differentiate:

\[
f(x)=x^{3}-a^{2}x
\]

so

\[
f'(x)=3x^{2}-a^{2}.
\]

The function is increasing where

\[
f'(x)\ge0.
\]

Therefore

\[
3x^{2}-a^{2}\ge0
\]

which gives

\[
x^{2}\ge\frac{a^{2}}{3}.
\]

Since \(a>0\),

\[
x\le-\frac{a}{\sqrt{3}}
\quad\text{or}\quad
x\ge\frac{a}{\sqrt{3}}.
\]

## Quickest method

Differentiate and solve the resulting quadratic inequality.

## Common trap

Choosing the middle interval, which is where the derivative is negative.
