## Worked solution

For the mass, \(T - 50 = 5.0 \times 0.80\), so \(T = 54\text{ N}\). The pulley is pulled down by two rope tensions, so the coupling must withstand \(2T = 108\text{ N}\).

## Quickest method

Find the rope tension for the accelerating mass, then double it for the force on the pulley support.

## Common traps

Choosing \(54\text{ N}\), which is the rope tension but not the total force on the coupling.
