## Worked solution

The joined edges make the circumference \(5\text{ m}\), so \(2\pi r=5\) and \(r=\frac{5}{2\pi}\). The height is \(10\text{ m}\). Thus \(V=\pi r^{2}h=\pi\left(\frac{5}{2\pi}\right)^{2}10=\frac{125}{2\pi}\).

## Quickest method

Identify the circumference as \(5\text{ m}\), not \(10\text{ m}\), then use \(V=\pi r^{2}h\).

## Common traps

Using the sheet height as the circumference, or forgetting to square the radius.
