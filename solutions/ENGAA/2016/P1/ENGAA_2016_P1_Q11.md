## Worked solution

The bearings form a triangle in which the horizontal separation \(AB\) is \(4\text{ km}\). Resolving the geometry gives \(BC = 4\cos 30^{\circ} = 2\sqrt{3}\text{ km}\).

## Quickest method

Draw the bearings from A and B, then use the 30 degree right-triangle relationship.

## Common traps

Using the bearing angles directly as interior angles without first drawing the north lines.
