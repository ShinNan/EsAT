## Worked solution

\[
4 + \frac{4-x^{2}}{x^{2}-2x}
=4+\frac{(2+x)(2-x)}{x(x-2)}
=4-\frac{x+2}{x}
=3-\frac{2}{x}
\]

## Quickest method

Rewrite \(x^{2}-2x\) as \(-x(2-x)\), cancel \((2-x)\), then combine with the \(4\).

## Common traps

Cancelling the factor correctly but losing the negative sign.
