## Worked solution

The area above the time axis is

\[
\frac{1}{2}\times20\times8 = 80\text{ m}
\]

The area below the time axis is

\[
\frac{1}{2}\times10\times2 = 10\text{ m}
\]

So the total distance is \(80+10=90\text{ m}\).

The displacement from the starting point is \(80-10=70\text{ m}\).

The average speed is

\[
\frac{90}{30}=3.0\text{ m s}^{-1}
\]

## Quickest method

Use graph area for total distance, signed area for displacement, then divide total distance by total time for average speed.

## Common traps

Using displacement instead of total distance for the average speed.
