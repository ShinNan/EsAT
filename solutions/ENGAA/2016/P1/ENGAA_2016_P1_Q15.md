## Worked solution

The area is \(\frac{1}{2}[(x-1)+(x+5)]x = 120\). This gives \(x^{2}+2x-120=0\), so \((x+12)(x-10)=0\). The positive value is \(x=10\), hence \(RS=x+5=15\text{ cm}\).

## Quickest method

Use the trapezium area formula and reject the negative root.

## Common traps

Solving for \(x\) but forgetting that the question asks for \(RS=x+5\).
