## Worked solution

Since \(x \propto \frac{1}{\sqrt{y}}\), \(x\sqrt{y}=k\). With \(x=8\) and \(y=9\), \(k=24\). When \(x=6\), \(6\sqrt{y}=24\), so \(\sqrt{y}=4\) and \(y=16\).

## Quickest method

Keep \(x\sqrt{y}\) constant.

## Common traps

Treating inverse square-root proportionality as inverse-square proportionality.
