## Worked solution

Check conservation of nucleon number and proton number. Diagram 2 balances: 235 + 1 = 96 + 137 + 3 and 92 = 38 + 54. The other diagrams do not conserve the required totals, so only diagram 2 is possible.

## Quickest method

For each diagram, add mass numbers and atomic numbers on both sides. Reject any diagram where either total changes.

## Common traps

Checking mass number only and not checking proton number, or missing emitted neutrons.

## Why the wrong options are wrong

- A. none of them: Diagram 2 is possible.

- B. 1 only: Diagram 1 does not conserve the required nuclear totals.

- D. 3 only: Diagram 3 does not conserve the required nuclear totals.

- E. 1 and 2 only: Diagram 1 is not possible.

- F. 1 and 3 only: Diagrams 1 and 3 are not both possible.

- G. 2 and 3 only: Diagram 3 is not possible.

- H. 1, 2 and 3: Only diagram 2 is possible.
