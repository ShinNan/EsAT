## Worked solution

The mass is \(\frac{30}{10}=3\text{ kg}=3000\text{ g}\). The material volume is \(10^{3}-(5.0^{2}\times10)=1000-250=750\text{ cm}^{3}\). The density is \(\frac{3000}{750}=4.0\text{ g cm}^{-3}\).

## Quickest method

Convert weight to mass, subtract the volume of the square hole, then use density = mass/volume.

## Common traps

Using the full cube volume and forgetting to subtract the hole.
