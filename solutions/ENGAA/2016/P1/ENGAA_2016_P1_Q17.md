## Worked solution

Start with \(a(3b^{2}-1)=b^{2}+2\). Then \(3ab^{2}-a=b^{2}+2\), so \((3a-1)b^{2}=a+2\). Therefore \(b=\pm\sqrt{\frac{a+2}{3a-1}}\).

## Quickest method

Collect the \(b^{2}\) terms before taking the square root.

## Common traps

Dropping the \(\pm\) when taking the square root.
