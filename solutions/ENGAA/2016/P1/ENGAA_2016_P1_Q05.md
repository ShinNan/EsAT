## Worked solution

Make the R parts match. Q:R = 5:2 is equivalent to 15:6, and R:S = 3:10 is equivalent to 6:20. Therefore Q:S = 15:20 = 3:4.

## Quickest method

Scale both ratios so R is 6, then compare Q and S directly.

## Common traps

Multiplying the visible Q and S numbers without matching the shared R term first.

## Why the wrong options are wrong

- A. 1:2: This does not match the combined ratio after equalising R.

- B. 2:1: Reverses the relative sizes.

- D. 3:25: Uses an incorrect combination of the ratio parts.

- E. 4:3: Reciprocal of the correct ratio.

- F. 25:3: Uses 5 and 10 without correctly matching R.
